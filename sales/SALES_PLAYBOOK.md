## Opening

## Discovery Questions

- What is the workflow today?
- Where does it break?
- What happens if this does not improve?
- Who owns the decision?
- What would make this worth changing now?

## Core Talk Track

1. Name the current pain.
2. Show the better workflow.
3. Prove why it is credible.
4. Connect to the buyer's urgency.
5. Agree on the next step.
