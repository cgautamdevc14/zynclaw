# Sales

Sales owns how the team explains, qualifies, demos, and advances the work with
customers or internal stakeholders.

Use this folder when a change needs revenue-facing handoff:

- `ICP.md`: ideal customer profile and qualification signals.
- `SALES_PLAYBOOK.md`: discovery, demo, and next-step guidance.
- `OUTREACH_SEQUENCE.md`: reusable outbound and follow-up copy.
- `DEMO_SCRIPT.md`: demo flow and proof points.
- `OBJECTION_HANDLING.md`: common objections and responses.
- `QUALIFICATION.md`: qualification questions and scoring.

## Sales Rules

- Sell the customer outcome, not the implementation detail.
- Qualify before demoing.
- Keep proof points specific.
- Feed product and marketing with objections heard in the field.
