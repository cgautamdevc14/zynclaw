# Qualification

## Fit Questions

- What engineering or PM work is repetitive enough to offload?
- How do you validate agent-generated work today?
- What local infrastructure do you already run?
- Who owns AI tooling decisions?
- What would a successful pilot prove?

## Scoring

| Area | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|------|---|---|---|
| Pain | No clear pain | Some pain | Urgent, repeated pain |
| Readiness | No infra | Partial infra | Ready inference host |
| Buyer | Unknown | Influencer | Decision owner |
| Validation | None | Manual only | Clear test harness |

## Next Step By Score

- 0-2: nurture or educate.
- 3-5: discovery and technical validation.
- 6-8: pilot plan.
