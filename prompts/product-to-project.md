# Product To Project Prompt

You are converting a product brief into a delivery plan.

Inputs:

- Product brief.
- Non-goals.
- Success metrics.
- Constraints.

Output:

- Ordered delivery slices.
- Owner/reviewer/QA owner suggestions.
- Risks and mitigations.
- Open decisions.
- First local-agent task candidate.

Rules:

- Keep the first slice small.
- Do not invent requirements.
- Mark unresolved questions clearly.
