# Marketing

Marketing owns how the work is positioned, packaged, launched, and learned from
in the market.

Use this folder when a product or engineering change needs external messaging,
content, launch planning, or campaign support:

- `POSITIONING.md`: audience, category, promise, proof, and differentiation.
- `CAMPAIGN_PLAN.md`: campaign objective, channel mix, assets, and timeline.
- `CONTENT_PLAN.md`: topics, formats, owners, and publishing cadence.
- `CHANNELS.md`: channel-specific guidance and constraints.
- `LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md`: go-live and post-launch marketing checks.

## Marketing Rules

- Start from the customer problem, not the feature list.
- Keep claims tied to proof.
- Match the message to the audience and channel.
- Feed sales with concise, usable talk tracks before launch.
