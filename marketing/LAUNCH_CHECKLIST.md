# Marketing Launch Checklist

## Before Launch

- [ ] Positioning reviewed.
- [ ] Audience and CTA are clear.
- [ ] Website or repo copy updated.
- [ ] Sales talk track shared.
- [ ] Support or internal FAQ prepared.
- [ ] Tracking links or metrics defined.

## Launch Day

- [ ] Publish primary asset.
- [ ] Share social posts.
- [ ] Notify internal team.
- [ ] Confirm links and CTAs work.
- [ ] Monitor replies, comments, and questions.

## After Launch

- [ ] Capture customer reactions.
- [ ] Report first results.
- [ ] Create follow-up content.
- [ ] Update sales objections and proof.
