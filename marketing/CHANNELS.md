# Channels

Use this as a lightweight channel guide.

## Website

- Lead with the category, audience, and outcome.
- Put proof near claims.
- Keep CTAs specific.

## Email

- One message per email.
- Make the subject line concrete.
- Keep the CTA obvious.

## LinkedIn

- Lead with a practical insight or concrete result.
- Avoid vague launch language.
- Use a clear next step.

## Sales-Assisted

- Turn launch messaging into talk tracks.
- Include objections and proof points.
- Keep enablement short enough to use on a call.

## Community

- Share lessons, setup details, or artifacts.
- Avoid sounding like an ad when the channel expects learning.
